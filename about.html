📘 About CyberSafe

CyberSafe is a cyber security awareness platform designed to educate students and internet users about online threats and safe practices.

🎯 Our Aim

To spread awareness about cyber threats and promote safe internet usage.

🎯 Objectives

Educate users about cyber risks, phishing attacks, password safety, and secure browsing habits.

🌍 Scope

This project can be used in schools, colleges, and organizations to reduce cyber crimes through awareness.

💡 Why Cyber Security Awareness Matters

🔐 Data Protection

Protect your personal and financial information.

⚠️ Prevent Attacks

Avoid phishing, malware, and scams.

🌐 Safe Internet

Build secure online habits for daily use.

👨‍💻 Project Team

This project is developed as part of a Cyber Security Awareness initiative.
