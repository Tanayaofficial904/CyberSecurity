🛡️ Cyber Security Awareness

Learn how to stay safe from online threats and protect your digital identity.

⚠️ Common Cyber Threats

Phishing

Fake emails or messages used to steal your personal data.

Malware

Malicious software that damages your system or steals data.

Ransomware

Locks your files and demands money to restore access.

Social Engineering

Tricks users into sharing confidential information.

💡 Safety Tips

🔑 Use strong passwords
📱 Enable 2FA
🚫 Avoid unknown links
🔄 Update software regularly
📧 Check email authenticity
🔒 Do not share OTP

📧 How to Identify Phishing

  • Check sender email address carefully
  • Look for spelling and grammar mistakes
  • Avoid urgent or threatening messages
  • Do not click suspicious links
